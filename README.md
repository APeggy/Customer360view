# Customer360view

### BUSINESS USE CASE: Building a Unified Customer 360 view for an E-commerce company
Background: An ecommerce company operates across multiple channels – website, mobile app, customer service, and order management system. However, the customer data is scattered across different systems, making it difficult to:
- Understand the full customer lifecycle
- identify high-value customers
- Personalize marketing
- Improve customer retention
The leadership team wants a single view of the customer that brings together:
- Profile information
- Purchase behabiour
- Payment history
- Customer support interactions
- Web and app activity
### Project Objective:
Create a customer 360 dashbord in Microsoft Fabric by integrating and cleaning data form multiple messy sources using lakehouse, pyspark and power bi to provide insights across the entire customer journey.
Data Sources
Source system origin – available in ADLS
KPI Tracked
- Total customers active customers with at least one transaction
- Average order value spend per order
- Repeat customer %, customers with 2+ orders
- Open ticket count – Unresolved customer complaints
- Churn risk – customers with past tickets and no recent orders
- Payment mode split % of credit card, UPI, Wallet, etc.
- Device preference – web vs mobile usage trend.

### Business Benefits
- Personalize marketing – segment customers by behaviour and preferences.
- Improve customer service – Agents can see full history before responding to queries.
- Smarter decision making – leadership can track KPIs and customer churn.
- 360 liecycle view – from signup -browse – order – pay - complaint
